Me

21/11/2020

Exit, coded by Bear!

You might not be aware, but during 2020 there was a major event that occured. So cataclysmic that the ripples can still be felt today. I am, of course, refering to me turning 41. I can tell by your stunned silence that you weren't aware, or, more likely, not expecting that answer. Well, let me explain.

You stayed? Well, that makes us friends and I don't want this friendship to set off on the wrong footing. Turning 41 itself wasn't the devastating event. ('I don't look 41? Thank you for saying so, you charmer.'). COVID-19 or Coronavirus was. And my birthday fell near the beginning of the pandemic here in the UK. A birthday on which I would have been performing on stage. Yes, I an Actor! The career I love doing, that is supplemented by working as a child practitioner for an events-based, childcare company. Two sectors where work dried up and disappeared faster than the truth from a politician. I found myself stuck at home, no work and no money. With no idea when or how the pandemic could end, I found myself to be much like Dante, at the crossroads of my life.
